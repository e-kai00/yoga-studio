Welcome to SATTVA studio

How to book a class

It's easy to book classes with us. Check our timetable and find a class and time that suits you! Give us a call or contact us via messengers on social platforms. If you are new to classes, let your instructor know when you arrive, and they will help you get started - all of our classes cater for all abilities, so you can work at your own pace.

What to wear

Choose comfortable, breathable non-restrictive clothing. Remember, our classes will get you moving! Don't forget to bring a towel to freshen up and a water bottle to hydrate (we have water fountains to refill any reusable water bottle).
